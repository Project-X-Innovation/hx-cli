Ownership

## Description
I want to start thinking about ownership. How do we bring ownership into Helix? In particular Helix for NetSuite but it transfers. How do we make sure there is ownership, right?

The simplest way to talk about ownership is whose neck is in the noose? That's a whole conversation. Is it the one who put in the ticket, the one who merged it, the one who deployed it? Maybe we need an entirely different way of deciding who whose head is on the noose or maybe that's just the totally wrong way to start thinking about ownership, whose neck is in the noose.

Maybe Helix takes ownership and we just, I don't know, someone has to verify. We don't want to go back to forcing product owners to hold back the process. We want Helix to be able to take ownership, right? Helix should be the one that allows humans. Brainstorm some ideas, right? I don't want to put a human's head in the noose. That defeats the purpose of Helix. Brainstorm the right way to do this. I have included documentation on our thesis and our manifesto for reference.

Take as much time as you need because this is really the core of our business. I don't expect it all to be done today. I don't think we're going to nail it but this is the first of many drafts so just start exploring some ideas and we'll continue from there.
